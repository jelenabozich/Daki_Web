    About Me

    My journey into physiotherapy began when my wife was diagnosed with MS. We were faced with seemingly hopeless situation and left without a satisfactory explanation or help from medical professionals. Difficulties we faced together has fueled my interest and education in the field as well as an intense drive to finding the most effective approach to treating individuals affected by MS. The result was a unique treatment, targeting both physical and psychological symptoms with great success rate.

    I learned the most significant education about this condition from his wife who has this diagnosis since 2000. and has been completely immobile since 2007. By looking at everyday problems and psychological turbulences and their impact on her condition, I established the therapy, which I applied since 2012 on other patients with a lot of success...

    Therapy

    In order to determine the necessary therapy and its approximate length, it is necessary to arange a time we can talk via Zoom in order to establish an anamnesis. The patient can then decide whether and when to apply the therapy that is being used here. It is noted that the camera that displays the patient should be distant from the patient as much as it takes to see the patient in its entirety (in a sitting or lying position). The therapy in the first week consists of four parts:
  • General introduction and getting to know patient's symptoms, psychological profile, the environment in which they live.
  • Psychotherapy, which changes the image of the patient's capabilities, the experience of external situations and events, changing of habits and perception, fulfilling the real personal needs.
  • Physical therapy aimed at acheiving phyical movements that are currently difficult or impossible for the patient because of their condition.

  • Note: the components of therapy or the order in which the components are conducted as presented here is just a general example. However, upon meeting each patient the emphasis is put on the individual needs and condition of each individual patient. In the first week, the therapy takes place 5 days in a row (Monday to Friday) in the same time period each day, which is agreed with the patient prior to the start of therapy. The duration of the daily therapy is 1 hour and a maximum of 1 hour and 30 minutes.

  • Optional: In the first week, the bio energetic therapy is incorporated with the other components for best results. Bio energetic therapy with which the aura (which represents an energy indicator) complements, balances and regenerates in the areas where the symptoms are expressed. The patient can opt out of this component of therapy by indicating so to the therapist.
    • It is especially emphasized that without the patient’s actual will to change the behavior and habits the therapy will not have complete success.
    That’s why the therapy is exclusively done and scheduled with a patient, not with a cousin, a friend or any other person. However, it would be desirable that patient's caregiver(s) and any other individuals involved in patient's daily life get involved in the therapy and attend the sessions as agreed upon with the Therapist.
